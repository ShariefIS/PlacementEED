This Elite Platform's story.

Placement Meant For You

Brain child of Mr. Mohammed Imran, Research scholar in Electrical and Electronics Engineering department having more than 5 years of experience as a placement coordinator for engineering students is to spread his experience and knowledge with all of you at Placemeant For You with his team!.


This is a freely accessible electronic platform for students for a thorough guide for their campus recruitment.We at 'PlaceMeant For You' help you get through your first and big step towards your goal, the aptitude round. Make yourself free of chains of worry as we provide you with the online test simulators identical to the actual aptitude process so that you feel comfortable and ready to take your big day head on. Further, we provide you with the patterns of aptitude papers of various companies, beneficial tricks and tips, mock papers that analyze your performance at the end along with explanation and help you convert your weakness into strength. Focusing upon each question and the topic it comes from, we aim at putting all our experience and heart into imparting the most authentic and detailed information.We provide you everything you think of. Our main goal is to making sure you ace the placement and succeed with flying colors in the recruitment process.
